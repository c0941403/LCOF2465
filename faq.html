Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Sajham Instant Pot or our rice cookers? Find the answers below!

What is the capacity of Sajham Instant Pot rice cookers?

Our rice cookers come in a variety of sizes ranging from 1.5 liters to 5 liters, catering to both small families and larger households.

Can I cook more than just rice in the Sajham Instant Pot?

Yes! Our rice cookers are versatile and can be used to prepare porridge, steamed vegetables, soups, and even cakes.

Are the inner pots non-stick?

Yes, all our inner pots are coated with a high-quality non-stick material, making them easy to clean and safe to use.

How long does it take to cook rice?

Cooking time varies depending on the type of rice. On average, our rice cookers take 20-30 minutes to cook white rice perfectly.

Do the rice cookers come with a warranty?

Yes, all Sajham Instant Pot rice cookers come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

How do I clean my Sajham Instant Pot rice cooker?

The inner pot is detachable and can be washed with warm soapy water. The outer surface can be wiped clean with a damp cloth.

Is there an automatic shut-off feature?

Yes, our rice cookers are equipped with an automatic shut-off feature to prevent overcooking and ensure safety.

Can I purchase replacement parts?

Yes, replacement parts like inner pots, lids, and measuring cups are available on our website or through authorized retailers.
